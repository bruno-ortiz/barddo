{% trans "Our proposal" %}

{% blocktrans %} I bet you, like us, follow famous manga or comics and many other styles published by known publishing houses, who've been doing this a long time. And there is nothing wrong with this, we love these works also. {% endblocktrans %}
{% blocktrans %} But why stick to this publishing style, only? We believe there are many talented artists in the world, and with the power of technology it's possible to give the visibility they deserve. {% endblocktrans %}
{% blocktrans %} The Barddo is a system of online publications focused on Independent Artists. With it, any artist, from any style, can offer their works for readers to evaluate. {% endblocktrans %}
{% blocktrans %} A ideia é bem simples, o artista publica sua obra, diz seu preço, e começa a compartilhá-la. Os leitores podem avaliar estes trabalhos de uma forma simples e rápida. Quanto melhor a avaliação, mais destaque as obras recebem. The idea is simple, the artist publish his work, at his price, and starts to share it. Readers can review these works simply and quickly. The better the review, the more the work receive more highlight on home page. {% endblocktrans %}
{% blocktrans %} And so, without discarding what exists today, our proposal is to provide the best of both worlds for our readers: the largest possible amount of quality works, offered by publishers well-known and established and help you discover new artists and great works, letting readers themselves have control over this, with no intermediaries. {% endblocktrans %}
{% trans "Imagine that!" %}

{% trans "Why?" %}

{% blocktrans %} Unlike current current model, in which some companies and individuals determine what is good enough for publication and influence the course of the published works, we believe that there's no one better than the readers themselves to decide the success of a publication . {% endblocktrans %}
{% blocktrans %} The big difference about Barddo is exactly this: freedom for the artist publish what they want, and direct contact with readers. {% endblocktrans %}
